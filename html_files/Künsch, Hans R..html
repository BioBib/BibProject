Honors

2000
IMS Fellow
"For contributions to probability theory and theoretical statistics, particularly the development of properties of Markov random fields and Gibbs distributions, and other aspects of spatial statistics; to robust methods for time series; to the bootstrap, especially block-based bootstrap methods for time series; and to properties of long-range dependent time series; and for services to the profession, particularly his work as an Editor of The Annals of Statistics and, as Associate Editor for periods starting in 1987."
